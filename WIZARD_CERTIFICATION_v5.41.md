# Wizard Builder Certification v5.41

## Coverage
- 2014: 9 races × 13 official published Wizard subclasses × 20 levels = 2,340 scenarios.
- 2024: 10 species × 4 Player’s Handbook Wizard subclasses × 20 levels = 800 scenarios.
- Total: 3,140 builder scenarios.

## Certified systems
- d6 Hit Die, INT/WIS saves, Intelligence spellcasting and edition-specific proficiencies.
- Full-caster spell-slot progression across levels 1–20.
- 2014 Intelligence modifier + Wizard level prepared-spell model.
- 2024 fixed prepared-spell table.
- Cantrip progression, six-spell starting spellbook and two free spells per Wizard level.
- Arcane Recovery budget and the level 5 maximum recoverable slot.
- 2014 subclass selection at level 2 and features at 2/6/10/14.
- 2024 subclass selection at level 3 and features at 3/6/10/14.
- Ritual Adept, Scholar, Memorize Spell, Spell Mastery, Epic Boon and Signature Spells checkpoints.

Warnings in the matrix identify species/subrace selections that remain Origin Builder responsibilities rather than Wizard blockers.
